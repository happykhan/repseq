Pareto curve: alpha trade-off guide
=====================================

The Pareto sweep does NOT tell you which alpha to use — that is a
scientific decision based on your study goals.

How to choose alpha:
  - alpha = 1.0  → pure phylogenetic diversity (ignore AMR/plasmid profile)
  - alpha = 0.0  → pure AMR/replicon diversity (ignore phylogeny)
  - alpha = 0.5  → equal weighting (default; good starting point)

Look for the elbow in pareto_plot.png: the point where adding more
phylo budget stops improving Faith PD meaningfully, or vice versa.
Use the random_mean_faith_pd_pct and random_mean_amr_pct columns in
pareto.tsv to check that your chosen alpha beats random selection on
both axes.
